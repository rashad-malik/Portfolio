About Me

I’m Rashad Malik, a London‑based data scientist with a background in mechanical engineering and seven years delivering analytics and SAP projects for FTSE‑100 clients. I love designing pipelines that turn chaotic enterprise data into clear, actionable insights.

At PwC I catalogued 800+ integrations and coordinated 1,400 transports for a global S/4HANA rollout, then pivoted to drive data‑centric asset‑management demos for energy utilities. Earlier at Bluefin I led legacy‑data migration for one of the UK’s first green‑field S/4HANA projects. My journey began on the turbine decks of RWE’s Pembroke power station, where I learned how data and engineering decisions intersect in the real world.
